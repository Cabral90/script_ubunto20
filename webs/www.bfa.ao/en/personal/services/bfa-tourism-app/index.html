Tourism Promotion

Guide


From 23 to 25 May 2019, Angola hosted the World Tourism Forum, an event which has as its purpose to promote investment and boost the tourism sector in Angola

In this way, BFA intends to further strengthen its support for the diversification of the economy, raising awareness of the potential of the tourist sector in Angola.
Angola is a world to discover and there is so much to explore! A unique culture, combined with incomparable natural beauty, customs and gastronomy, make tourism a focus with everything to make it a success. We have invested in this sector because we are committed to taking Angola further. 

Explore and learn more about the best of the 18 provinces of Angola. BFA offers you a unique tourist guide with information from our branches and national tourist points through a web site  https://www.bfaturismo.co.ao/ and a BFA Tourism App.

The focus on Tourism is one of the great opportunities that our country has for the future. Together, we will bring the world to Angola.
